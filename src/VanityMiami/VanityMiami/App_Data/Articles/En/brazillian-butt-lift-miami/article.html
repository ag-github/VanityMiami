Brazilian Butt Lift Miami Introduction

The Brazilian Butt Lift procedure is a form of buttocks augmentation that not only reshapes, but also enhances the size of a patient’s buttocks so that it better fits the figure and provides an attractive, prominent rear end. In order to perform this surgery, fat from other areas of the body including back, abdomen, thighs and hips is removed through liposuction. The fat cells are purified and only the best tissue is used for injection into the buttocks at various depths for a natural look and feel.

The buttocks is an area that naturally tends to show signs of aging. Over the years, it can begin to drop and fail to keep the look people desire to have. With the Brazilian Butt Lift, individuals can fight this natural aging effect. The procedure is a safe form of plastic surgery that has the benefit of using the patient’s own tissue for long-lasting beautiful results.

Variations of the Brazilian Butt Lift

Techniques

There is a great deal of variability in the aesthetic outcomes produced by the surgeons, but each one brings their own feeling to the surgery and produces a unique result. The various buttock techniques are as follow:

Miami Thong Lift

Brazilian Butt’s improved procedure, also known as Miami Thong Lift, leads in innovation as a fat grafting procedure. By making an incision down your center buttock crack, and liposuctioning the fullness that exists, the resulting scar remains largely hidden in the natural crevasse. The length of the scar depends on the fullness of the inner fold of the gluteus. It also depends on how much fat must be removed to correct the unpleasing aesthetic appearance. The Miami Thong Lift involves more than just buttock enhancement. It involves sculpting, contouring and defining shape. Fat grafting also plays a role in the procedure. The fat adds volume to the hips and buttock region. Sutures also play a major component. Specifically designed threads lift and round the buttocks. The knots in the threads, or sutures, must be covered by special cones which add comfort and ease to the presence in the body.

The Butterfly Lift

The Miami Thong Lift alone might not be enough to perk up the rear end. A procedure called the Butterfly Lift may be able to bring about the desired results. This procedure results entails a more extensive scarring, which can be hidden along the top of the bikini line. The incision allows for the lifting of excess skin. The length of the scar and its extension towards the front will depend on how much work needs to be done.

 

The Inner and Posterior

Depending on the laxity of the inner and outer leg, an inner leg lift might be required. This occurrence becomes especially common in people who have lost a great amount weight or simply have developed very loose skin. The incision takes place along the groin crease, which aids in hiding it. The scar anchors to the bone which prevents the scar from migrating down the thigh into visibility. In case of extreme laxity, the scar would have to reach around from the groin into the crease of the gluteus followed by the lifting of the posterior and interior part of the leg.

The C-Lift

The C-lift encompasses the Miami Thong Lift, the Butterfly Lift, posterior Leg Lift, and tightening up the entire rear region. The scar is in the form of a C-shape that creates a further lift in the upper/outer part of the buttocks. Patients who have excess skin or fat around the entire buttock area may be ideal candidates for the Miami C-Lift.

Brazilian Butt Lift Procedure Description

There are general concepts surgeons must follow in order to obtain good reproducible results. The Brazilian Butt Lift is performed as an outpatient procedure under general or local anesthesia. The fat harvested from areas such as thighs, abdomen, back, or hip are cleansed and purified to isolate the highest quality fat cells to be used for injection to the buttocks. By placing the fat cells close to a blood supply and in between layers of muscle, the harvested cells would likely survive and not be reabsorbed by the body. Fat also contains stem cells which can turn into new tissue, so fat transfer creates new collagen which improves the skin texture and thickness. By injecting the fat at various depths, the patient will receive the highest permanent absorption rate and a firm bottom. Very small incisions are made into the buttocks to serve as the entry point for fat transfer. Areas of the buttock with low volume can be restored with fat, while lifting and tightening the skin. This new fullness restores the buttocks to a more youthful shape. The entire procedure may take from 90 minutes to four hours. The time frame depends upon multiple factors, including the amount of fat to be removed, treatment area, and the patients desired shape and size of buttocks.

What to expect after the Brazilian Butt Lift Surgery

Following the surgery, doctors will have the patient wear a special compression garment that will help the healing process and support the buttocks tissue. They should expect to refrain from putting much weight on their behinds for a few weeks following the procedure. Many providers say not to sit for prolonged periods of time for a full two weeks. The pressure may displace the fat or cause more of it to be absorbed. If they must sit, the patient should sit on a padded or cushioned surface and not on hard surfaces. They also prefer to restrict patients from sleeping flat on their back, as these positions place a lot of pressure on the buttock areas, and this might suppress the desired outcome. Patients should be able to return to work, assuming it is not a job that involved rigorous physical activity, within 2 weeks. After about 2 months, they should experience a full recovery from any pain, swelling, bruising, and soreness. Patients should be able to resume intense physical exercise with sufficient amount of time.

On average, one should wait around one month to begin to feel normal, two months before resuming workout routines, and six months to see final results.
