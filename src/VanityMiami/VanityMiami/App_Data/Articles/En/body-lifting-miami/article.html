Recovery process after a Body Lift

  • You need to stay at the clinic the first day after the surgery, because you will have the best postoperative care by our nurses and you may apply for intravenous pain medication.
  • In case of any discomfort or pain you should contact your doctor which is available for you 24 hours a day or you might need to take some painkiller because pain and discomfort is normal after any surgery.
  • Bandages are applied immediately after the plastic surgery to reduce swelling.
  • It is necessary that a family member accompanies you the first days of the after the surgery because is normal to have a swollen abdomen and an achy body after surgery.
  • If you must have a drain after the surgery, this will be taken off after 5 to 7 days after the surgery.
  • The stitches are removed 7 to 10 days after body lift surgery.
  • An adhesive tape is used during the first two weeks to help keep the wound closed.
  • It is important to rest at home but also to walk several times a day to help speed the recovery process and, reduce the risk of blood clots in the legs.
  • You must not play sports and exercise until after six weeks post-surgery, because this is a very complex surgery.
